Tips for a great highlight video
→Keep it between 3–5 minutes. Coaches watch hundreds of videos.
→Start with your best moments in the first 30 seconds — that's what gets watched most.
→Include your name and sport at the beginning of the video.
→Good video quality matters. Avoid very shaky or dark footage.
→Make sure your video is set to Public or Unlisted on YouTube.
